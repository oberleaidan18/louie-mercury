ABOUT ME.

Based out of Richmond, VA, I am a freelance music producer and performer. From a young age music has always been a passion of mine. I started out taking piano lessons at the age of 9 and haven’t stopped playing since. I also picked up the drums soon after with that now being my primary instrunment.

I have a background studying music production and jazz percussion at Shenandoah University. Using my experience, I am passionate about helping musicians turn their artistic vision into a finished product that the public will enjoy. From creative suggestions to handling the technical side of recording, I’m here to help.


Let’s work together and create something unique!

VENTURES.

MUSIC PRODUCTION.

I am passionate about collaborating with musicians and helping them bring their songs to life. There’s nothing like taking an idea and turning it into a polished track. I always strive to do the artist and the song justice with my production. This means working with the artists vision in mind. A will also assist in giving creative suggestions to anyone who is open to that.

LIVE PERFORMANCE.

Music is all about collaboration and connection. There’s a reason they say music is the universal language. To me, there’s nothing like playing live with other musicians to an audience of people. The energy that comes from it it undescribable. This is why I’m so passionate about music and why I fell in love with it in the first place. When it come to performing, my main instrunment is the drums/percussion with piano being my second. I have had the oppurtinity to play with a variety of groups all accross different styles. I am studied in jazz percussion and also have experience in contemporary, rock, and hip hop. My variety of musical styles gives me an musical voice that is truly unique.
